Alleppey, Kerala

About Alleppey

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is famous for its picturesque backwaters, houseboat cruises, and lush green landscapes. It is often called the "Venice of the East" and is a top destination for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty in Kerala.

Highlights

  • Houseboat stays on the backwaters
  • Alleppey Beach
  • Snake boat races
  • Traditional Kerala cuisine

Visitor Tips

  • Best time to visit: November to February
  • Try a houseboat cruise for a unique experience
  • Respect local traditions and environment
