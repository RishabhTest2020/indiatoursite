Jodhpur, Rajasthan

About Jodhpur

Jodhpur, the Blue City, is famous for its blue-painted houses, mighty Mehrangarh Fort, and vibrant bazaars. It is a cultural and architectural gem of Rajasthan.

Highlights

  • Mehrangarh Fort
  • Jaswant Thada
  • Umaid Bhawan Palace
  • Clock Tower and Sardar Market

Visitor Tips

  • Best time to visit: October to March
  • Explore the old city on foot
  • Try local snacks and sweets
