About City Info India Travels

CITY INFO INDIA TRAVELS is an organization approved by the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India, dedicated to making your journey across India seamless, memorable, and enriching.

We specialize in curating personalized travel experiences to India's most iconic destinations, including Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, Rajasthan, Kashmir, Himachal, Uttrakhand, and beyond. Whether you are seeking cultural immersion, adventure, or relaxation, our expert team crafts itineraries tailored to your interests and needs.

  • Comprehensive Travel Bookings: Hotels, flights, and car rentals for a hassle-free experience.
  • Expert Local Guidance: In-depth knowledge of Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, Rajasthan, Kashmir, Himachal, Chandigarh, Amritsar, and more.
  • Flexible Car Rentals: One-way and round-trip options with professional drivers.
  • Authentic Cultural Experiences: Explore India's rich heritage, cuisine, and traditions with our curated tours.
  • 24/7 Customer Support: Our team is always available to assist you before, during, and after your trip.

Our mission is to provide accurate information, transparent pricing, and the best customer service, ensuring every traveler enjoys a safe and unforgettable journey in India.
